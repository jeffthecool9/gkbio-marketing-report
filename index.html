High Risk

Ad Account Restriction

Facebook may restrict or disable the ad account at any time due to policy violations, unusual spending patterns, or automated reviews. A single account shutdown halts all 17 active campaigns and disrupts revenue flow immediately.

IMPACT: 100% campaign downtime if single account is restricted
Solution

Multi-Account Risk Coverage

  • Purchase 3–5 additional ad accounts via verified Business Manager profiles to distribute campaign load
  • Split campaigns across accounts — top performers (Campaign 11, 15) on primary, testing on secondary accounts
  • Warm up new accounts gradually — start at RM30/day, scale 20% every 3 days to avoid flags
  • Keep backup creatives & audiences saved — instant relaunch capability if one account goes down
  • Diversify BM ownership — use separate verified business pages per account group
Revenue Loss

Campaigns Below 1.0× ROAS

Campaigns 03 (0.93×), 13 (0.84×), and 14 (0.98×) are spending more than they earn. Combined spend of RM754.86 with negative or breakeven returns — money is being burned without profit.

IMPACT: ~RM754.86 in inefficient spend across 3 campaigns

Kill, Pivot, or Restructure

  • Pause immediately — any campaign below 0.9× ROAS after RM150+ spend is a confirmed loser
  • Reallocate budget to winners — shift freed budget to Campaign 11 (RM120/day → RM180/day) and Campaign 15 (RM100/day → RM150/day)
  • Test new angles — if restarting, use completely different creative + audience combos, don't repeat the same setup
  • Set ROAS kill threshold — auto-rule: pause any ad set below 1.0× ROAS after RM200 spend
Tracking Gap

Missing Purchase Data

Solution

Fix Attribution Pipeline

  • Set up offline conversion uploads — for WhatsApp/messaging sales, manually or via CRM upload purchase events back to Meta
  • Use UTM parameters — tag every campaign link so Google Analytics captures source even if pixel fails
  • Audit event match quality — aim for Event Match Quality score > 6.0 in Events Manager
Inefficiency

Expensive Messaging Conversations

Campaigns 04 (RM75.09), 13 (RM88.50), 17 (RM71.93), and 01 (RM56.21) are paying 2–3× more per conversation compared to the best performers at RM25–30. This is wasted budget on poor-quality audiences or fatigued creatives.

IMPACT: Paying RM88.50 vs RM25.31 best rate = 3.5× overpay
Solution

Audience & Creative Optimization

  • Refresh creatives every 7–14 days — ad fatigue causes CPR to spike; rotate 3–5 creative variants per ad set
  • Build lookalike audiences from Campaign 11 & 15's converters — 1%, 2%, 3% LAL from purchase events
  • Narrow targeting — exclude non-buyers, past clickers who didn't convert, and low-quality placements (Audience Network)
  • A/B test ad formats — carousel vs video vs static; test WhatsApp vs Messenger as destination
  • Dayparting — analyze when conversions happen, schedule ads during peak buying hours only
Structure

Mixed Budget Strategy (CBO vs ABO)

Some campaigns use daily campaign budget (RM50–120/day) while others use ad set budget. This inconsistency makes it hard to compare performance, control spend, and let Meta's algorithm optimize delivery effectively across ad sets.

IMPACT: Suboptimal budget allocation and inconsistent data
Solution

Standardize to CBO Structure

  • Move to Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO) for all scaling campaigns — let Meta auto-distribute to winning ad sets
  • Use ABO only for testing — when testing new audiences/creatives, use ad set budget at RM30–50/day to control variables
  • Set minimum spend per ad set in CBO — ensure each ad set gets enough budget to exit learning phase (50 conversions/week)
  • Naming convention — prefix campaigns: [CBO-SCALE], [ABO-TEST], [ABO-RETARGET] for clear tracking

4 PHASES
01

Foundation & Tracking

Fix what's broken before scaling

Week 1–2

Pixel & CAPI Setup

Install Conversions API (server-side) alongside Meta Pixel. Target Event Match Quality > 6.0. Test with Pixel Helper on every conversion page. Ensure purchase, add-to-cart, and initiate-checkout events all fire correctly.

Offline Conversion Pipeline

For WhatsApp/messaging sales, build a manual or CRM-based upload workflow — export purchases weekly, match to click IDs, upload to Meta for attribution. This recovers the hidden ROI on campaigns 02, 04, 05, 17.

Multi-Account Setup

Provision 3–5 verified ad accounts across 2–3 Business Managers. Warm each with RM30/day spend for 7 days. Assign Campaign 11 & 15 to primary, testing campaigns to secondary accounts.

Naming Convention

Standardize: [OBJECTIVE]_[AUDIENCE]_[CREATIVE]_[DATE]. Example: MSG_LAL1%-Purchasers_VideoA_Jun26. Enables instant performance filtering and pattern recognition across accounts.

02

Creative & Audience Testing

Systematic discovery of winners

Week 2–4

Creative Testing Matrix

Run 3 hooks × 3 formats × 2 CTAs = 18 creative variants. Test in ABO at RM30/day per ad set. Kill any variant below 1.0× ROAS after RM150 spend. Graduate winners to CBO scaling campaigns.

Audience Layering

Layer 1: Lookalike 1% from Campaign 11 & 15 purchasers. Layer 2: Interest-based (health, supplement, wellness). Layer 3: Broad targeting (let Meta's AI find buyers). Test each layer independently first.

Ad Format A/B Tests

Test: Video testimonial vs Carousel (before/after) vs Static image with bold claim. Also test WhatsApp vs Messenger as messaging destination — conversion rates differ significantly by channel.

Landing & Messaging Flow

Optimize the post-click experience. Script WhatsApp auto-replies, qualification questions, and closing sequences. Aim to reply within 5 minutes — response time directly impacts conversion rate from conversation to purchase.

03

Scaling & Budget Allocation

Amplify proven winners systematically

Week 4–8

Vertical Scaling

For campaigns above 2.0× ROAS: increase budget 20% every 3 days. Never double budget overnight — it resets learning phase. Campaign 15 (3.21× ROAS) is the first scaling candidate: RM100 → RM120 → RM150 → RM180/day.

Horizontal Scaling

Duplicate winning ad sets into new campaigns targeting different audience segments. Clone Campaign 09's creative (5.99× ROAS) into 3 new ad sets with LAL 1%, LAL 2%, and interest-based audiences. Run at RM80/day CBO.

Budget Reallocation

Apply the 70/20/10 rule: 70% budget to proven winners (ROAS > 2.0×), 20% to testing new creatives/audiences, 10% to experimental formats. Review weekly. Current RM364K spend suggests daily run rate — redistribute accordingly.

Automated Rules

Set up Meta automated rules: (1) Pause if cost/result > RM60 after 3 days, (2) Increase budget 15% if ROAS > 3.0× for 3 consecutive days, (3) Notify if frequency > 3.0, (4) Pause if reach < 100/day (audience exhaustion).

04

Advanced & Retention

Maximize lifetime value + protect margins

Week 8+

Retargeting Funnel

Build 3-tier retargeting: (1) Engaged with ad but didn't message — show social proof/testimonial ads, (2) Started conversation but didn't buy — show limited-time offer, (3) Past purchasers — upsell/cross-sell complementary products. Budget: RM30–50/day.

Customer LTV Analysis

Track repeat purchase rate from messaging customers. If a customer has 2+ purchases, you can afford higher CPA on acquisition. Calculate: (Avg Order Value × Purchase Frequency × Retention Period) to set true CPA ceiling.

Cross-Platform Expansion

Once Facebook ROAS is optimized to 3.0×+ blended, test TikTok Ads and Google Ads for additional reach. Use Facebook's winning creatives as a starting point. Allocate 15% of total budget to new platforms initially.

Weekly Performance Ritual

Every Monday: review spend, ROAS, CPR across all accounts. Kill losers (ROAS < 1.0× after RM200), scale winners (+20%), launch 2 new test ad sets. Track blended ROAS target of 2.5×+ across portfolio.
